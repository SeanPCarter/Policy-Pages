Data Deletion Instructions

App: My N8N Workflow
Last updated: August 17, 2025

If you would like to request deletion of data processed by this app, please do the following:

  1. Send an email to sndvlnvt@hotmail.com with the subject line “Data Deletion Request — My N8N Workflow”.
  2. Include a link to your Facebook profile and, if available, links to posts or comments you want removed from processing logs.
  3. We will confirm receipt within 7 days and complete deletion within 30 days, removing relevant workflow logs and Twilio message records under our control.

You may also disconnect the app at any time by removing Page subscriptions (via Facebook Page settings) or deactivating the n8n workflow.

For any questions, contact: sndvlnvt@hotmail.com.
